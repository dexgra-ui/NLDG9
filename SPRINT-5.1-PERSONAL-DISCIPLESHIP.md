# Sprint 5.1 — Personal Discipleship

## Theme
Your Journey with God

## Delivered
- My Journey dashboard with continue reading, recently viewed, favorites, completed studies, saved notes, prayer list, current reading plan, and recommended next study.
- Expanded local user profile with optional photo, spiritual interests, preferred Bible translation, theme preference, and weekly reading goal.
- Seven reading plans with daily progress, resume position, and completion tracking.
- Personal study journal with prayer reflections, questions, highlighted Scriptures, and note export.
- Prayer journal with categories, answered status, attached Scriptures, and linked studies.
- Favorites across all Content Library types, including No Labels Games and ministry resources.
- Achievement badges for study completion, reading streaks, Scripture exploration, prayer, learning, and family leadership.
- Enhanced recommendations based on interests, history, favorites, Scripture signals, audience, and completion.
- Offline caching for the discipleship dashboard, notes, prayer data, reading progress, and study tools.

## Architecture boundary
All personal discipleship information is stored locally in the browser because NLDG9 currently runs as a static GitHub Pages application. Secure user accounts and cross-device synchronization require a protected backend in a later sprint.
