# Sprint 6.5 — Ministry Tools

## Theme
Equip the Church

## Delivered
- Sermon builder with Scripture, big idea, purpose, outline, application, and closing.
- Lesson builder with objectives, teaching content, questions, and next steps.
- Printable handout maker with print preview.
- Presentation outline builder with export.
- Curriculum planner for multi-week discipleship paths.
- Event planner with date, location, purpose, schedule, and ministry needs.
- Volunteer scheduler with roles, assignments, and instructions.
- Searchable saved-project library with preview, export, duplicate, and delete.
- Local-device persistence and offline caching.

## Ministry principle
These tools organize a leader’s prayerful preparation. They do not replace Scripture study, theological accountability, discernment, or the work of the Holy Spirit.

## Architecture boundary
Projects are stored in browser localStorage. Collaboration, church-wide templates, permissions, cloud storage, calendar integration, volunteer notifications, and cross-device access require a secure backend and authenticated accounts.
